Pizza Recipe

Ingredients

Basic Pizza Dough

  • 2 tsp. sachet dried yeast
  • 1 cup (250 ml) lukewarm water
  • 2 2/3 cups (400 g) plain flour, plus extra to dust
  • 2 tsp. salt
  • 3 tbsp. (¼ cup) olive oil

Capricciosa Topping

  • 1 cup (250 ml) pizza sauce
  • 1 2/3 cups (150 g) Perfect Italiano™ Perfect Pizza cheese
  • Handful of sliced button mushrooms
  • A few thin slices ham, torn into pieces
  • 4 green pitted black olives, sliced
  • Handful jarred artichokes in brine, drained, quartered

Method

  1. Step 1

    2. To make the dough, whisk yeast and water in a heatproof jug. Stand for 5 minutes or until frothy.

  2. Step 2

    3. Place flour and salt in a large bowl. Make a well in the centre. Pour in the yeast mixture and oil. Using your hands or a spatula, mix the dough until it comes together and is smooth. Turn out onto a lightly floured work surface and knead briefly.

  3. Step 3

    4. Place dough in a lightly oiled bowl and cover with cling wrap. Set aside in a warm place to rise for 30 minutes to an hour until it doubles in size.

  4. Step 4

    5. Turn dough out onto a lightly floured surface and knead gently for another 5 minutes or until smooth. Divide dough in half, wrap one half in plastic wrap and freeze for another use. Roll out remaining dough half to create a 30 cm x 20 cm oval. Sprinkle a little flour on the bench before rolling to prevent dough from sticking. Transfer base to a baking paper lined tray.

Capricciosa Topping

  1. Step 1

    2. Preheat oven to 230°C / 210°C fan-forced.

  2. Step 2

    3. Spread pizza base with the pizza sauce. Scatter with a small handful of Perfect Pizza cheese. Top with mushroom, ham, olives and artichokes. Scatter over the remaining cheese.

  3. Step 3

    4. Bake for 20-25 minutes or until cheese is melted and golden and base is crisp.
